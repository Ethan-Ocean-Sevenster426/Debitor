1. What the system does

The FSA Debtor System gives the finance team a fast, local view of who owes the business money, splits that work between administrators, and keeps a complete record of every reminder and follow-up call on each invoice — ready to print for collections or legal use.

Invoice data is copied from Xero into a local SQL Server database on a schedule, so the pages load instantly and don't depend on Xero being reachable. The app never sends reminders — Xero sends those; the app reflects them and lets your team act on them.

3. Logging in

  • Go to the app URL and sign in with your email address and password.
  • Change your password after first login (Users page → your account → Reset password).
  • Use Logout (top-right) when finished.

4. Where the data comes from (Xero sync)

  • A scheduled job pulls open invoices from Xero every hour into the local database.
  • The Refresh now button on the Debtors Action Page triggers a sync immediately.
  • Xero allows ~5,000 API calls per day. If that limit is reached, the sync pauses cleanly and resumes automatically once the limit resets (overnight) — no action needed.

5. The Dashboard

As a Super Admin you see:

  • System Overview — total outstanding (closed businesses excluded), number of debtors, and clickable scorecards: Calls required, Missed calls, At final demand, At handover, and unallocated companies. Clicking a scorecard opens the Debtors Action Page filtered to those invoices.
  • Outstanding by invoice month — a line chart of how the outstanding book is spread across months.
  • Invoices by age — a pie chart of how many invoices sit in each ageing band.
  • Administrator Tracking — a table and bar chart showing clients allocated, outstanding, calls required, missed calls, final demand and handover counts per administrator, so you can see where everyone is tracking.

View an administrator's workload

Use the "View administrator" dropdown at the top of the dashboard (or click an administrator's name in the tracking table). The dashboard then shows that person's outstanding, call list, missed calls and charts — exactly what they still need to phone and collect. Choose "— Whole system —" to return.

6. Allocating companies to administrators

  • On the Debtors Action Page, each company row has an "Allocated to" dropdown. Pick an administrator to assign that company; pick "Unallocated" to clear it.
  • Administrators only see (and are measured on) the companies allocated to them.
  • Allocations survive the hourly Xero sync.

7. The Debtors Action Page

  • Ageing tiles (Not Yet Due, 0-30, 31-60, 61-90, 91-120, 120+) double as filters — click one to show only that band. "Total Outstanding" clears the filter.
  • Search by debtor name or invoice number.
  • Administrator filter (Super Admin) — narrow the whole page to one administrator's book.
  • Scorecard filters — arriving from a dashboard scorecard shows only Calls required / Missed / Final demand / Handover invoices.
  • Click a company row to expand it into a statement of its outstanding invoices, with the Contact details panel and a per-invoice Report link.
  • Each row shows who it's allocated to, a 📞 Call due badge, and a ⚠ Missed call badge where relevant.
  • Use the Report button (end of each row) for a full company statement. Mark closed (Super Admin only) closes a business.

8. Reminder cadence, calls & missed calls

Xero sends reminders on this cadence (reflected in each invoice's lifecycle):

  1. Due in 7 days
  2. Due date
  3. 7 days after due date
  4. 14 days after due date — administrator should phone the client
  5. 21 days after due date — administrator should phone the client
  6. 60 days — Final demand
  7. 65 days — Action / handover process
  • Call required: an invoice that has reached the 14/21-day reminder. Click 📞 Call on the invoice — you'll be prompted to enter what was discussed; this logs the call and adds it to the invoice's lifecycle.
  • After logging a call, that invoice's prompt clears for 7 days so it drops off the to-do list (it stays on screen — you don't lose your place).
  • Missed call: an invoice that reached the 21-day reminder with no call ever logged — flagged ⚠ Missed. The Administrator Tracking table shows missed-call counts per administrator.

9. Invoice lifecycle, comments & documents

  • Click any invoice in a company's statement to open its lifecycle — every reminder Xero sent, every call logged, and every comment, in date order.
  • Add a comment with a date/time, free text, and one or more document uploads (proof of payment, correspondence, etc.). Comments become part of the lifecycle.
  • Comments and calls save without reloading the page, so you keep your place on the client.

10. Closing & reopening debtors

  • Mark closed moves a business to the Closed Debtors page and removes its money from the dashboard totals and the open list.
  • On the Closed Debtors page, Reopen moves a business back to active collection.

11. Reports (legal / collections)

  • Single invoice report — the "Report" link on an invoice (or "Printable report" in its lifecycle) opens a print-ready record of that invoice and its full lifecycle.
  • Company report — the "Report" button on a company row produces a statement of all its outstanding invoices, each with its lifecycle.
  • Use the browser's Print / Save as PDF to hand the report to a customer, attorney, or for your records.

12. Managing users

  • Open Users (top menu). Add a user with a name, email and role; set or reset passwords; deactivate users who leave.
  • Only Super Admins can manage users.
  • New administrators start with no companies — allocate companies to them on the Debtors Action Page.
